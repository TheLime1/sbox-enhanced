# Privacy policy

Effective July 15, 2026

s&box Enhanced does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal data. It contains no analytics, advertising, telemetry, user accounts, or background network requests, and it does not use browser storage.

Creator statistics are read from the sbox.game page already open in the browser. JSON and CSV exports are generated and downloaded locally; the extension does not upload their contents.

The extension provides visible links to Steam, s&box watch, s&boxDB, GitHub, and other sbox.game pages. Those sites are opened only after the user clicks a link and are governed by their own privacy practices.

Questions or concerns can be reported through the project's [GitHub issue tracker](https://github.com/TheLime1/sbox-enhanced/issues).
